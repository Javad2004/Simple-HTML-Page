Orange Lovers

An orange is a fruit of various citrus species in the family Rutaceae (see list of plants known as orange); it primarily refers to Citrus × sinensis, which is also called sweet orange, to distinguish it from the related Citrus × aurantium, referred to as bitter orange. The orange is a hybrid between pomelo (Citrus maxima) and mandarin (Citrus reticulata). The chloroplast genome, and therefore the maternal line, is that of pomelo.The sweet orange has had its full genome sequenced.
wikipedia orange fruits

In 2019, 79 million tonnes of oranges were grown worldwide, with Brazil producing 22% of the total, followed by China and India

The etymology of the word “orange” goes back to the Sanskrit word for “orange tree,” nāranga, though that word could be from the Dravidian (another South Asian language) word for “fragrant.”
